POFID's governance mechanism and community self-government instrument

  • DMW Asset Review
  • VM team
  • Platform mechanism governance
  • Technical community
For any asset type that is considered to be used as a pledge in the DMW, it is necessary to collect votes from the community users who hold NT$. However, before voting is initiated, it is necessary to proceed the preliminary examination and get the admission from the assets management qualification committee.
The Value Watchers (the VM team) are responsible for over watching the value of each asset in the DMW, and adjusting value fluctuations by making a value guard smart contract and evaluating the risks.
Users can initiate and participate in the POFID platform governance through the governance mechanism, from setting fees to DMW pledge rate and asset access objection.
POFID is an open source project, which encourages its community members to participate and introduce more derivative financial products.
