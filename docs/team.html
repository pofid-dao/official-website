Meet the Team

POFID’s initial core team has completed all the work related to V1.0, and newer versions after V1.0’s are open for technical contributions by the global developer communities. Technology governance and decision-making power will be shared between the “twelve knights of the roundtable”. They will have an election once every six months, and the first-ranking “knight” will have two votes in decision making.
